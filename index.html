Odds Sync
Fetches live odds from 40+ bookmakers via The Odds API. Calculates model vs market value gaps and writes them to your fixture cards. Run after the Soccer Engine to get value grades.
What Odds Sync adds to each fixture:
⚡ Best available odds across 40+ books · Best bookmaker per outcome
📊 Fair implied probabilities (margin removed) · Market margin %
🎯 Model edge = your prob minus market prob · EV% calculation
🏆 Value grade A/B/C shown on fixture cards · Best value market flagged
